My Sincere Apology

My Dearest [Name],

I pen this missive with a burdened heart, for it has come to my attention that my conduct—though borne of earnest admiration—has wounded thee. Should you have felt that I regarded you with anything less than the highest esteem, or that my intentions were of a hurried and unbecoming nature, I prostrate myself before thee in deepest contrition.

Verily, I have held you ever since our first 2 meetings in such regard that I deemed all others are dull-witted and dumb for failing to recognize your worth. In my haste to declare my interest, lest those fools come to their right senses and permit thee to slip away, I fear I erred grievously. For this, I am profoundly remorseful.

You are deserving of every courtesy, every patience, and every noble gesture. If I have strayed from this path, I humbly ask for your forgiveness. My mind and soul shall know no rest until this shadow between us is dispelled.

I'm not making excuses for my behavior. I take full responsibility for my actions, and I am willing to do whatever I can to repair the trust I've broken between us.

With the utmost sincerity and regret,

Adedolapo
